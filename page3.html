Indian Cuisine

Cuisine based on different regions of India

Northern India

Northern India includes a couple of these states: Rajasthan, Panjab, Uttarakhand, Madhya Pradesh.

Some of food items they are known for are:

  • Chole Bhature

  • Palak Paneer

  • Baigan Bharta

  • Lassi

Eastern India

Eastern India includes a couple of these states: Arunachal Pradesh, West Bengal, Bihar, Tripura.

Some of food items they are known for are:

  • Rasgulla

  • Rice and fish

  • Jhaal Muri

  • Pakhala

Southern India

Southern India includes a couple of these states: Kerela, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh.

Some of food items they are known for are:

  • Dosa

  • Utthapam

  • Idli

  • Kaapi

Western India

Western India includes a couple of these states: Goa, Maharashtra, Gujarat.

Some of food items they are known for are:

  • Dhokla

  • Zunka Bhakri

  • Gatte ki Sabzi

  • Bebinca
